What the service is

We are a media agency delivered through software. Once you connect Shopify and your ad accounts, we read your data and publish a personalised media strategy within three working days. After that, the dashboard refreshes daily and we send a short list of recommendations each week. We also offer a managed buying service across programmatic channels and, as that service expands, across Meta Ads and Google Ads. The boundary between what we read and what we do is set in writing on the data & privacy page and at the moment of each connection.

Connected platforms

When you connect Meta Ads, Google Ads, or Google Analytics to Passo, you grant us a limited, revocable permission to access your account on a read-only basis for as long as the connection is live. You retain ownership of every connected account and the data inside it. You can disconnect any source from the dashboard at any time. We will revoke the relevant OAuth token with the upstream platform on the same day.

If you later instruct us in writing to manage a connected ad account on your behalf, the scope of access expands to include the write operations needed to run that service. We will set out the specific scopes before they take effect, and we will never enable a write scope without your explicit instruction.

Subscription and prepaid media balance

The service is provided on a monthly subscription. The subscription covers the strategy report, the dashboard, and the weekly recommendations. Your monthly fee is the fee shown on the pricing page and on the Shopify App Store listing at the moment you install. We may change the subscription fee on thirty days' written notice; if you do not wish to continue at the new fee, you can uninstall the app before the change takes effect.

If we are buying media on your behalf, you also maintain a prepaid media balance that funds those buys. You see the amount paid in and the amount remaining at all times. There is no auto top-up. We never charge media spend to a card we have not been instructed to draw on. If you uninstall the app, we refund any unused media balance to the same payment method within thirty days.

Satisfaction refund in your first month

If you are not happy with Passo at any point during your first calendar month, ask for a refund of the platform fee you paid for that month and we will issue it. There are no eligibility checks, no forms, and no approval queue. The refund covers your platform fee for the month. It does not cover media spend, because the inventory was delivered to real audiences. The refund is available once per merchant and is processed in the dashboard. If no invoice has yet been raised when you ask, no money needs to move and we record the request as resolved.
