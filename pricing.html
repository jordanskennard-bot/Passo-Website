Pricing

A subscription plus a prepaid media balance. No hidden fees.

Pricing is finalised at Shopify App Store launch and published in full on the listing. A monthly subscription covers the platform. A prepaid media balance funds the buys we make on your behalf, and you only ever see what you paid in and what is remaining.

What you can expect

The shape of the bill.

Monthly subscription
A fixed monthly fee that covers the strategy report, the dashboard, and our weekly recommendations. Cancellable at any time from your dashboard settings.
Prepaid media balance
You top up a balance and we draw against it to fund the media buys we make on your behalf. You see what you paid in and how much is remaining at all times. There is no auto top-up; you stay in control.
No long-term contract
Month-to-month. Pause or cancel any time. If you uninstall the Shopify app we refund any unused media balance to the same payment method.

Specific numbers are intentionally absent from this page until the App Store listing publishes, so you only ever see one canonical price. If you would like a heads up the moment the listing goes live, join the waitlist and we will email you the install link.
